# Risk, Safety, and Exam Reasoning

Driver exams often test judgment more than isolated memory.

## Safety Pattern

Good answers usually include:

- slowing down when visibility or control is limited;
- yielding when conflict is possible;
- avoiding sudden movement;
- using signals and mirrors;
- maintaining space.

## Reasoning Trap

Do not choose the most aggressive answer just because it is technically possible. Choose the action that is legal, controlled, and predictable.
