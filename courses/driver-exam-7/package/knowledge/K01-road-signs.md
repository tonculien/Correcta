# Road Signs and Signals

Road signs help drivers predict hazards, obey laws, and make safe decisions before a dangerous situation develops.

## Sign Categories

- Regulatory signs tell drivers what they must or must not do.
- Warning signs alert drivers to upcoming hazards or changes.
- Guide signs provide directions, routes, or service information.

## Exam Reasoning

When a question asks about a sign, do not only identify its shape or color. Ask what action the sign requires from the driver.
