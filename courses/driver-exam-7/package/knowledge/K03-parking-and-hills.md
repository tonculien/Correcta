# Parking, Hills, and Vehicle Control

Parking rules often test whether the driver understands how the vehicle may move if brakes fail.

## Hill Parking

- Uphill with curb: turn wheels away from the curb.
- Downhill with curb: turn wheels toward the curb.
- Without curb: turn wheels toward the edge of the road.

## Vehicle Control

If a vehicle malfunctions, the driver should avoid panic, maintain control, signal if possible, and move away from traffic when safe.
