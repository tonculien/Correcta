# Right-of-Way and Intersections

Right-of-way rules are designed to prevent conflict, not to reward whoever moves first.

## Core Ideas

- Yield when another road user has legal priority.
- At uncontrolled intersections, slow down and prepare to yield.
- Emergency vehicles with active sirens/lights require drivers to pull over safely.
- Pedestrians in marked crosswalks usually require extra caution and yielding.

## Exam Reasoning

Look for the safest lawful action. If two answers seem possible, prefer the one that reduces conflict and preserves control.
