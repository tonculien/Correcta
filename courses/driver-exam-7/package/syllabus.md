# 7-Day Driver Exam Prep

This Correcta package simulates a short driver-exam preparation course. It focuses on practical exam reasoning rather than memorizing isolated facts.

## Course Goals

By the end of the course, the student should be able to:

- Recognize major categories of road signs and signals.
- Explain basic right-of-way rules at intersections.
- Apply hill parking and vehicle-control rules.
- Reason through driver-exam style scenarios.

## Course Rhythm

- Days 1–2: Road signs and signals.
- Days 2–4: Right-of-way and intersections.
- Days 4–6: Parking, hills, and control.
- Days 6–7: Final practice test.

## Submission Style

Each assignment asks the student to submit a short `.txt` or `.md` response. The response should explain reasoning, not just give short answers.
